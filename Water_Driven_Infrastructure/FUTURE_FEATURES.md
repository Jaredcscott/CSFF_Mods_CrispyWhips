# Water Driven Infrastructure - Future Feature Ideas

## Core Water Systems

### Water Management
- **Water Reservoir System** — Large-scale water storage that can be filled during rainy seasons
- **Aqueduct Extensions** — Modular water channels to direct flow over terrain obstacles
- **Water Distribution Hub** — Central junction to split water flow to multiple devices
- **Water Intake Structures** — Capture runoff from rain or higher terrain

### Water Quality
- **Water Filtering System** — Purify water before use in sensitive operations
