# Herbs and Fungi — Player Guide

A complete how-to for every item, plant, and structure in the mod.

---

## Getting Started

Most content is foraged — start exploring forest biomes and caves (Old Hollow, Shady Thicket, Still Hollow) to discover mushrooms and herbs. Build a **Drying Tray** early (Support crafting tab) so nothing rots before you can process it.

**Suggested early priorities:**
1. Find Yarrow → dry → grind → Yarrow Tea (free wound treatment)
2. Build a Drying Tray and collect herbs
3. Plant Hemp Seeds when you have fertile soil
4. Research Oil Press once you find Oil Press Utensils

---

## Mushrooms

All foraged mushrooms are found in forests. Most spawn on the surface; Truffles must be dug up.

### Edible Mushrooms

| Mushroom | How to Eat | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| **Chanterelle** | Raw or cooked | Golden funnel-shaped; common, safe raw |
| **Giant Puffball** | Raw or cooked | Large white sphere; very filling |
| **King Oyster** | Raw or cooked | Dense and meaty; high protein |
| **Golden Oyster** | Raw or cooked | Nutty cashew-like flavor |
| **Shiitake** | Raw or cooked | Immune-boosting; best cooked |
| **Chicken of the Woods** | **Must cook** | Toxic raw — place on any fire source first |
| **Morel** | **Must cook** | Toxic raw — always cook before eating |
| **Black Trumpet** | Raw or cooked | Rare, gourmet smoky flavor |

**Cooking mushrooms:** Place a fresh mushroom on any lit fire (campfire, stove, copper stove) and watch the cook progress. Remove when done. Cooked versions are safer to eat and often more nutritious.

### Medicinal Mushrooms

These are primarily used for grinding and brewing rather than eating directly.

**Lion's Mane**
- Chew raw for a mild boost, or dry → grind → brew into **Lion's Mane Tea** for a cognitive boost.
- Found in forests; relatively uncommon.

**Reishi**
- Bitter taste raw. Best dried → ground → brewed into **Reishi Tea** for immune support and stress relief.
- Long spoilage time — one of the few mushrooms you don't need to rush to process.

### Truffle

The rarest and most valuable mushroom.

- Found underground — look for **birch trees** and use the **Dig for Truffles** action on the ground beneath them.
- Spoils in 1 day (fastest of any mushroom) — dry immediately or use right away.
- Eat raw for a huge hunger boost, or dry → slice → cook with fat (see Truffle Cooking below).
- Press in the Oil Press to produce **Truffle Oil** — a luxury cooking ingredient.

**Truffle Cooking:** When you heat Dried Truffle Slices alongside **Fat**, **Fat Chunks**, **Butter Chunks**, or **Milk Butter** in the same cooking container, the truffle consumes one fat and transforms into a **Cooked Truffle** instead of burning to ash. Without fat in the same container, dried truffle slices will char.

### Death Cap

**Do not eat.** Eating even one Death Cap will cause severe poisoning and is likely fatal.

**Safe uses:**
- **Death Cap Tincture** (craft at a kiln) — a bitter amatoxin extract that kills gut parasites when consumed in small doses.
- **Poison a weapon** — drag a Death Cap onto a spear, axe, or blade to coat it in contact poison.

Handle them like tools, not food.

## Herbs & Roots

### Yarrow

The primary wound-treatment herb. Found in meadows and forest edges.

**Spoilage:** ~3 in-game days fresh → 60 days dried.

1. Place fresh Yarrow on a **Drying Tray** until fully dried (~8 dry ticks).
2. Drag a grinding tool (or use a Rotary Quern / Tea Blending Station) onto the dried yarrow → **Ground Yarrow**.
3. Options:
   - **Eat** the powder directly for wound-healing effects.
   - **Apply to Wound** action (3 daytime) — reduces infection and bleeding on open wounds.
   - **Brew into Yarrow Tea** — drag ground yarrow onto a hot bowl of water (50%+ heat) to create medicinal tea.

### Ginseng

An energy and stamina herb. Found in shaded forest areas.

**Spoilage:** ~5 in-game days fresh → 60 days dried. Lasts longer than other herbs.

1. Dry on a Drying Tray (~16 dry ticks — takes longer than yarrow).
2. Grind into **Ground Ginseng**.
3. Options:
   - **Eat** for a significant energy/stamina boost.
   - **Brew into Ginseng Tea** — drag ground powder into a hot bowl of water.

### Wild Ginger

A spice and food ingredient.

**Spoilage:** ~1 day fresh — dry quickly.

- **Fresh:** Eat for hunger + warming effect.
- **Sliced:** Cut fresh ginger to create slices for recipes.
- **Dried:** Greatly extends shelf life. Good for trading and long-term storage.

## Drying & Preservation

### Drying Tray

Crafted from the **Support** tab. Holds 6 items simultaneously.

1. Place fresh herbs, mushrooms, hemp flowers, ginger, or meat on the tray.
2. The tray auto-ticks — each item dries over time.
3. When an item reaches its dry threshold, it transforms into its dried form (Dried Yarrow, Dried Shiitake, etc.).

**Tip:** Dry quickly — most fresh mushrooms spoil within 2–3 days. A Drying Tray can save items you can't process immediately.

### Drying Stack

Craft from the **Furniture** tab. A placed structure that holds **up to 3 Drying Trays** vertically.

- Place the stack indoors or outdoors.
- Slot 3 trays into it for 18 drying slots total.
- Dismantle when empty to recover 4 Planks.

### Wooden Pantry

A sealed cool-storage cabinet that slows spoilage to **75% of normal**. Built from the Furniture tab; place it indoors.

- Not animal-safe — foxes, badgers, and other wildlife can raid it.
- Best for storing dried herbs, preserved foods, and anything with a moderate shelf life.

## Hemp Farming

Hemp provides fiber (from stalks), food (from seeds), smokeable material (from flowers), cooking fat (as butter), and press oil.

### Getting Seeds

- Forage in meadows and forest edges.
- Harvest from a mature Hemp Plant (yields more seeds than you planted).

### Planting

1. Research **Hemp Field** or **Plant Hemp Seeds** blueprint (Farming Agriculture tab) after finding seeds.
2. Blueprints unlock in tilled soil or garden plots — the mod also injects hemp into vanilla plantation cards automatically.
3. Plant 2 seeds + a hoe.

### Harvesting

When the plant or field matures, harvest:
- **Hemp Flower** — dry, then grind for smokeable powder
- **Hemp Stalks** — use the **Extract Fibers** action to get plant fiber
- **Hemp Seeds** — replant or press into oil

### Hemp Processing Paths

| Input | Process | Output |
|-------|---------|--------|
| Hemp Flower | Dry on tray | Hemp Flower Dried |
| Hemp Flower Dried | Grind | Hemp Flower Powder |
| Hemp Flower Powder | Load into Herb Pipe | Herb Pipe (Hemp) |
| Hemp Flower Powder + Fat | Cook together | Hemp Butter |
| Hemp Seeds | Oil Press | Hemp Seed Oil |
| Hemp Stalks | Extract Fibers | Plant Fiber (×4) |

## Herb Pipe

A reusable carved wooden pipe for inhaling herbal powders.

**Loading the pipe:** Drag a herbal powder onto the pipe to load it. Powders and their effects:

| Powder | Mood | Relaxation | Notes |
|--------|:----:|:----------:|-------|
| **Hemp Flower Powder** | Moderate | Moderate | Balanced |
| **Fairyweed Powder** | High | Moderate | Best mood boost |
| **Appleweed Powder** | Moderate | Moderate-High | Balanced |
| **Frostleaf Powder** | Moderate | High | Best relaxation |

**Using the pipe:** Drag a fire source (any lit flame) onto the loaded pipe to smoke it. Quality degrades with each use — a fresh pipe holds 3 uses.

**Empty:** Use the "Empty Pipe" action to recover the unloaded pipe and discard leftover powder.

## Mushroom Log Cultivation

Grow your own mushrooms indoors from inoculated logs.

**Supported species:** Shiitake, Lion's Mane, Reishi, Chicken of the Woods, Golden Oyster, King Oyster.

**Process:**
1. Find at least 5 of the target mushroom type (you need them to inoculate the log).
2. Research the matching **Inoculate Mushroom Log** blueprint (Farming Agriculture tab). Blueprint becomes researchable after you possess the mushroom type.
3. Craft from: 1 log + 1 spoon auger + 5 matching mushrooms + 1 wood shavings.
4. The log becomes a **Growing Fungal Log** — keep it indoors or sheltered while it colonizes (about 5 in-game days).
5. Harvest the ready log for fresh mushrooms.

**Tip:** Inoculated logs are an excellent source of medicinal mushrooms (Reishi, Lion's Mane) that are harder to find in the wild.

## Oil Press

A multi-stage heavy workstation for pressing oils from seeds, truffles, and herbal plants.

### Building the Oil Press

The press is built in parts — each component has its own blueprint:

| Component | Where to Research |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Oil Press Legs | Farming Materials tab |
| Oil Press Frame | Farming Materials tab |
| Press Bed Frame | Farming Materials tab |
| Pressing Screw | Farming Materials tab |
| Pressing Bag | Farming Materials tab |
| Press Plate | Farming Materials tab |
| Oil Press Utensils | Farming Materials tab |
| Prepared Press Bag | Farming Materials tab |

Once all components are in hand, research and build the **Oil Press Kit** (Furniture tab), then place it to deploy the structure.

**Shortcut:** The **Oil Press** character-creation perk starts you with a Kit — skips the entire build chain.

### Oil Press Recipes

Drag the ingredient onto the placed Oil Press to see and start the recipe:

| Ingredient | Output |
|------------|--------|
| Hemp Seeds | Hemp Seed Oil |
| Truffle | Truffle Oil |
| Fairyweed | Fairyweed Herbal Oil |
| Appleweed | Appleweed Herbal Oil |
| Frostleaf | Frostleaf Herbal Oil |
| Hemp Flower | Hemp Herbal Oil |

Herbal oils provide the same mood/relaxation effects as their pipe equivalents — an alternative for players who prefer not to smoke.

## Pickle Vat (Long Fermentation)

Preserves fermentable foods for months using salt brine.

### Building the Vat

1. Craft the **Unfired Pickle Vat** (Utensils tab).
2. Fire it in a kiln → **Pickle Vat** (item, CardType 0).

### Making Brine

1. Fill the pickle vat (item form) with water from any container.
2. Drag **Salt** onto the water-filled vat → water transforms into **Pickle Brine** in-place.

### Fermenting

1. Add fermentable ingredients to the vat. Ginger, Turnroot, and Fireroot are tagged fermentable by this mod; pickle blueprints in the crafting journal gate specific content recipes (Pickled Vegetables, Pickled Meat, Pickled Mushrooms, Pickled Frogs).
2. **Cap with Clay Bowl** → Closed Pickle Vat.
3. Drag water onto the closed vat to **Seal** it → Sealed Pickle Vat (placed structure).
4. Wait ~7 in-game days for fermentation to complete.
5. When the timer expires → **Ready Pickle Vat**. Harvest to spawn the pickled goods plus an empty fired vat for reuse.

**Important:** The pickle blueprints require brine — water alone won't start fermentation.

## Medicinal Teas

Brew ground medicinal powder in hot water.

**Required:** A bowl, cup, or kettle of water with **50%+ heat** (fully boiling or very hot).

| Tea | Ingredient | Effect |
|-----|-----------|--------|
| **Yarrow Tea** | Ground Yarrow + hot water | Wound healing, antiseptic, bleeds reduction |
| **Ginseng Tea** | Ground Ginseng + hot water | Energy, mental clarity, fatigue reduction |
| **Reishi Tea** | Ground Reishi + hot water | Immune support, stress relief |
| **Lion's Mane Tea** | Ground Lion's Mane + hot water | Cognitive boost |
| **Sleep Tea** | (combined herbs) | Improved sleep, lower stress |
| **Stimulant Tea** | (combined herbs) | Focus and energy boost |
| **Anti-Nausea Tea** | (combined herbs) | Settles stomach |

**Brewing:** Drag the ground herb powder onto a hot water container (must be heated on a fire — campfire, copper stove, tea kettle, etc.).

## Other Medicinal Items

**Herbal Salve** — Craft from herbs + fat. A topical wound treatment applied directly to injuries.

**Herbal Tincture** — An alcohol-based herbal extract; more potent than tea in some situations.

**Pain Relief Powder** — Ground Yarrow and vanilla Frostleaf Powder blended into an analgesic; reduces pain stats.

**Death Cap Tincture** — Made from Death Cap mushrooms. Kills gut parasites when consumed carefully. Risky — manage dosage.
